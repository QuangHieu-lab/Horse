# Horse Racing Tournament Management System
-
Horse racing is a sports and entertainment activity that involves many participants, such as horse owners, jockeys, referees, organizing committees, and spectators. In each tournament, various activities need to be managed, including registration for competition, race scheduling, result tracking, rankings, and outcome prediction.
- 
The management of horse racing tournaments is currently still manual and lacks synchronization, causing the processes of managing information, schedules, results, and predictions to be time-consuming, prone to errors, and creating difficulties for both the organizing committee and participants.
- 
Horse Owner

Register an account to join the system
Register horses to participate in tournaments
Manage horse information
Hire/select a jockey for the horse to participate in a race
Manage the horse's jockey list, confirm jockey participation in a race
View the horse's racing schedule, confirm horse participation in a race
View race information
Track race results, rankings, and prize money for the horse


Jockey

Register a jockey account
Receive riding invitations from horse owners
Confirm or decline race participation
View the list of assigned races and information about the horse to be ridden
Track the racing schedule
Track personal race results, rankings, and individual achievements


Race Referee

Inspect horse information before a race
Monitor the race
Record & handle violations
Confirm race results
Prepare race reports/minutes


Spectator

View tournament information and racing schedule
Follow live race results and rankings
Predict race outcomes
Track prediction results
Receive notifications for prediction reward


Admin

Manage user accounts
Assign roles and permissions
Manage horse racing tournament information, schedule races, and organize races and heats/rounds
Approve participation registrations
Manage the list of competing horses and jockeys
Assign referees
Announce race results
Manage result predictions
